# Pyruvate Kinase M2 Activator (TEPP-46)

**Category:** supplement
**Evidence Grade:** D
**Risk Profile:** medium

## Description
Research compound that activates pyruvate kinase M2, shifting cellular metabolism from aerobic glycolysis (Warburg effect) back to oxidative phosphorylation. Emerging interest in longevity community for metabolic reprogramming and potential anti-cancer properties.

## Mechanisms of Action
- PKM2 tetramerization (activating glycolysis completion)
- Warburg effect reversal
- Metabolic reprogramming
- mTOR pathway modulation
