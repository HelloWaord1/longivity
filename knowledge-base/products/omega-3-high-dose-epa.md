# Omega-3 (High-Dose EPA)

**Category:** supplement
**Evidence Grade:** A
**Risk Profile:** low

## Description
High-dose EPA (eicosapentaenoic acid) focused omega-3 for cardiovascular and anti-inflammatory benefits beyond standard fish oil. Icosapent ethyl (Vascepa) is the prescription form. Higher EPA:DHA ratio appears more cardioprotective than balanced formulations.

## Mechanisms of Action
- Triglyceride reduction
- Anti-inflammatory (resolvin and protectin production)
- Membrane fluidity optimization
- Endothelial function improvement

## Dosage
- **Standard:** 2-4g EPA/day
- **Range:** 1-4g EPA/day
- **Notes:** EPA-focused (3:1 or higher EPA:DHA ratio). Vascepa (icosapent ethyl) is purified EPA â€” Rx. OTC options: concentrated EPA fish oil. Take with fatty meals for absorption.

## Key Findings
- REDUCE-IT: 4g/day icosapent ethyl reduced cardiovascular events 25% (NEJM)
- Omega-3 index >8% associated with 5 years greater life expectancy
- Higher EPA specifically reduces cardiovascular inflammation beyond DHA

## Interactions
- Blood thinners (additive antiplatelet effect)
- High-dose aspirin

## Side Effects
- Fishy burps
- GI discomfort
- Easy bruising at very high doses

## Contraindications
- Fish/shellfish allergy
- Active bleeding
