# Fisetin

**Category:** supplement
**Evidence Grade:** C
**Risk Profile:** low

## Description
Most potent natural senolytic in screening studies

## Mechanisms of Action
- Senolytic
- Anti-inflammatory
- Antioxidant
- Neuroprotective

## Dosage
- **Standard:** 500mg intermittent (2 days/month)
- **Range:** 100-500mg
- **Notes:** Found in strawberries. Intermittent high-dose may be more effective as senolytic.

## Key Findings
- Most potent natural senolytic in screening studies
- Extended healthspan in mice by ~10%
- AFFIRM-LITE trial underway for COVID long-haulers
