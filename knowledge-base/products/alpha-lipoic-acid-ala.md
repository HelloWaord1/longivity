# Alpha-Lipoic Acid (ALA)

**Category:** supplement
**Evidence Grade:** B
**Risk Profile:** low

## Description
Universal antioxidant — works in both aqueous and lipid environments

## Mechanisms of Action
- Mitochondrial cofactor
- Antioxidant (both fat and water soluble)
- Glucose metabolism

## Dosage
- **Standard:** 600mg/day
- **Range:** 300-1200mg/day
- **Notes:** R-form (R-ALA) is the bioactive isomer. Take on empty stomach.

## Key Findings
- Universal antioxidant — works in both aqueous and lipid environments
- ALADIN trial: improved diabetic neuropathy
- Recycles other antioxidants (vitamins C, E, glutathione)
