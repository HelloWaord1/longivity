# Fiber (Prebiotic Blend)

**Category:** supplement
**Evidence Grade:** A
**Risk Profile:** low

## Description
Higher fiber intake consistently linked to lower all-cause mortality

## Mechanisms of Action
- Short-chain fatty acid production
- Gut microbiome diversity
- Blood sugar regulation

## Dosage
- **Standard:** 25-35g/day total fiber
- **Range:** 20-50g/day
- **Notes:** Mix of psyllium, inulin, acacia fiber. Most people get <15g. Increase slowly.

## Key Findings
- Higher fiber intake consistently linked to lower all-cause mortality
- Feeds beneficial gut bacteria, produces butyrate
- Regulates blood sugar, reduces cardiovascular risk
