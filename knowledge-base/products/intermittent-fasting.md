# Intermittent Fasting

**Category:** protocol
**Evidence Grade:** B
**Risk Profile:** low

## Description
Activates autophagy after 16-24h fasting

## Mechanisms of Action
- Autophagy induction
- Insulin sensitivity
- mTOR inhibition
- Ketogenesis

## Dosage
- **Standard:** 16:8 (16h fast, 8h eating window)
- **Range:** Various: 14:10 to OMAD
- **Notes:** Start with 14:10, progress to 16:8. Time-restricted eating. Not for everyone.

## Key Findings
- Activates autophagy after 16-24h fasting
- Improved insulin sensitivity and metabolic markers
- NEJM review: benefits for aging, cancer, cardiovascular disease
