# Lithium (microdose)

**Category:** supplement
**Evidence Grade:** C
**Risk Profile:** low

## Description
Regions with higher lithium in water have lower dementia rates

## Mechanisms of Action
- Autophagy induction
- GSK-3β inhibition
- Neuroprotective
- Mood stabilization

## Dosage
- **Standard:** 1-5mg/day (lithium orotate)
- **Range:** 0.5-20mg/day
- **Notes:** Microdose — NOT therapeutic psychiatric dose. Lithium orotate form.

## Key Findings
- Regions with higher lithium in water have lower dementia rates
- Extends lifespan in C. elegans and Drosophila
- Neuroprotective at microdoses in human studies
