# Pregnenolone

**Category:** supplement
**Evidence Grade:** C
**Risk Profile:** medium

## Description
The "mother hormone" — precursor to all steroid hormones including DHEA, progesterone, cortisol, testosterone, and estrogen. Levels decline dramatically with age. Supports cognitive function, mood, and hormonal balance.

## Mechanisms of Action
- Steroid hormone precursor (all pathways)
- GABA-A receptor modulation
- NMDA receptor potentiation
- Neurosteroid — enhances memory and cognition

## Dosage
- **Standard:** 30-50mg/day
- **Range:** 10-100mg/day
- **Notes:** Start low (10mg). Can convert to multiple downstream hormones. Monitor with blood tests. Take in morning.

## Key Findings
- Levels decline ~60% between ages 35-75
- Improved memory and cognitive performance in small human trials
- Enhanced myelination and neuroplasticity in preclinical studies

## Interactions
- Hormone-sensitive conditions
- Other hormone supplements (DHEA, progesterone)

## Side Effects
- Acne
- Hair loss (if converts heavily to androgens)
- Mood changes
- Overstimulation

## Contraindications
- Hormone-sensitive cancers (breast, prostate)
- Pregnancy
- Endometriosis
