# Shilajit (Purified)

**Category:** supplement
**Evidence Grade:** C
**Risk Profile:** low

## Description
Mineral-rich biomass exudate from Himalayan rocks, used in Ayurvedic medicine for millennia. Contains fulvic acid, dibenzo-alpha-pyrones, and 85+ minerals. Enhances mitochondrial CoQ10 function, increases testosterone, and supports mineral absorption. Must be purified and tested for heavy metals.

## Mechanisms of Action
- Fulvic acid — mineral absorption enhancement
- Mitochondrial electron transport support (CoQ10 synergy)
- Testosterone and fertility support
- Adaptogenic — stress resistance

## Dosage
- **Standard:** 250-500mg/day (purified extract)
- **Range:** 100-1000mg/day
- **Notes:** MUST be purified and tested for heavy metals. PrimaVie is most studied branded extract. Take in morning. Traditional use: dissolved in warm milk or water.

## Key Findings
- Increased testosterone by 20% in healthy men (Clinical Ther & Pharm, 2016)
- Enhanced CoQ10 function in mitochondria (synergistic effect)
- Improved sperm count and motility in infertile men

## Interactions
- Iron supplements (may increase absorption)
- Diabetes medications

## Side Effects
- GI discomfort
- Heavy metal toxicity (unpurified products)
- Allergic reaction (rare)

## Contraindications
- Iron overload (hemochromatosis)
- Active gout
