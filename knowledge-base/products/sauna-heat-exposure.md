# Sauna (Heat Exposure)

**Category:** protocol
**Evidence Grade:** B
**Risk Profile:** low

## Description
4-7x/week sauna: 40% lower all-cause mortality (Laukkanen et al.)

## Mechanisms of Action
- Heat shock proteins
- Cardiovascular conditioning
- Growth hormone release
- Detoxification

## Dosage
- **Standard:** 15-20min at 80-100°C, 4-7x/week
- **Range:** 10-30min
- **Notes:** Finnish sauna studies. Infrared sauna at lower temps also beneficial. Hydrate well.

## Key Findings
- 4-7x/week sauna: 40% lower all-cause mortality (Laukkanen et al.)
- Increases heat shock proteins — cellular protection
- 2-3x growth hormone increase after single session
