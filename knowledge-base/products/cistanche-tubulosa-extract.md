# Cistanche tubulosa Extract

**Category:** supplement
**Evidence Grade:** C
**Risk Profile:** low

## Description
Desert-dwelling parasitic plant used in Traditional Chinese Medicine for over 1,800 years as a "yang tonic." Modern research reveals potent anti-aging properties including telomerase activation, neuroprotection, and stem cell proliferation. Known as the "ginseng of the desert."

## Mechanisms of Action
- Telomerase activation
- BDNF and NGF upregulation
- Klotho protein expression increase
- Stem cell proliferation

## Dosage
- **Standard:** 100-300mg/day (standardized extract)
- **Range:** 100-500mg/day
- **Notes:** Standardized to echinacoside and acteoside content. Take in morning. Used in Chinese longevity formulas for centuries.

## Key Findings
- Extended lifespan in C. elegans by 20-30%
- Increased telomerase activity in human peripheral blood mononuclear cells
- Improved cognitive function and reduced brain aging markers in animal studies

## Interactions
- Immunosuppressants (immune-modulating effects)

## Side Effects
- Mild GI discomfort
- Allergic reaction (rare)

## Contraindications
- Autoimmune conditions
- Pregnancy
