## Dosage
- **Standard:** 200mg/day (ubiquinol form)
- **Range:** 100-600mg/day
- **Notes:** Ubiquinol > ubiquinone for absorption. Essential if on statins.

## Key Findings

- Essential for anyone on statin medications
