# Oura Ring

**Category:** device
**Evidence Grade:** B
**Risk Profile:** low

## Description
Smart ring wearable tracking sleep stages, HRV, resting heart rate, body temperature, blood oxygen, and activity. Provides daily readiness and sleep scores. Compact form factor with research-grade accuracy for sleep tracking.

## Mechanisms of Action
- Photoplethysmography (PPG) for heart rate and HRV
- Accelerometer for sleep staging and activity
- Temperature sensor (deviation tracking)
- SpO2 monitoring

## Dosage
- **Standard:** Continuous wear, charge every 4-7 days
- **Range:** Daily use
- **Notes:** Gen 3. Best sleep tracker available in wearable form. HRV and temperature trends most valuable for longevity tracking. Pairs with app for insights.

## Key Findings
- 94.1% agreement with polysomnography for sleep staging
- Detected COVID-19 onset 2.75 days before symptoms via temperature deviation
- HRV tracking correlates with autonomic nervous system health and recovery
