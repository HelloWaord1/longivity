# NR (Nicotinamide Riboside)

**Category:** supplement
**Evidence Grade:** B
**Risk Profile:** low

## Description
Increases NAD+ levels by 40-90% in humans

## Mechanisms of Action
- NAD+ precursor
- Sirtuin activation
- Mitochondrial function

## Dosage
- **Standard:** 300mg/day
- **Range:** 250-1000mg/day
- **Notes:** ChromaDex (Niagen) is the most studied form

## Key Findings
- Increases NAD+ levels by 40-90% in humans
- Improved hepatic lipid metabolism
- Well-tolerated in clinical trials
