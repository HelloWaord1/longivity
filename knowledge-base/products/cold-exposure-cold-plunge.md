# Cold Exposure (Cold Plunge)

**Category:** protocol
**Evidence Grade:** B
**Risk Profile:** low

## Description
2-3x increase in norepinephrine (mood, focus, alertness)

## Mechanisms of Action
- Brown fat activation
- Norepinephrine release
- Anti-inflammatory
- Hormesis

## Dosage
- **Standard:** 2-5min at 10-15°C, 3-5x/week
- **Range:** 1-15min
- **Notes:** Start gradual. End showers cold. Deliberate cold exposure — not hypothermia.

## Key Findings
- 2-3x increase in norepinephrine (mood, focus, alertness)
- Activates brown adipose tissue — metabolic benefits
- Anti-inflammatory: reduced IL-6 and CRP in regular practitioners
