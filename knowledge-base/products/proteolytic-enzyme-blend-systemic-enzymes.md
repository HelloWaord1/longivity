# Proteolytic Enzyme Blend (Systemic Enzymes)

**Category:** supplement
**Evidence Grade:** C
**Risk Profile:** low

## Description
Systemic enzyme formulations (bromelain, trypsin, chymotrypsin, papain, rutin) taken on empty stomach for systemic anti-inflammatory, fibrinolytic, and immune-modulating effects. Used in German and European integrative medicine for decades. Wobenzym is the most studied brand.

## Mechanisms of Action
- Systemic proteolysis (inflammatory protein degradation)
- Fibrinolytic activity
- Immune complex clearance
- Anti-edema and analgesic effects

## Dosage
- **Standard:** 3-6 tablets on empty stomach, 2-3x/day
- **Range:** Product-specific
- **Notes:** MUST take on empty stomach (1hr before or 2hr after food). Enteric coating protects from stomach acid. Wobenzym N most studied. German clinical standard.
