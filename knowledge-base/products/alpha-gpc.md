# Alpha-GPC

**Category:** supplement
**Evidence Grade:** B
**Risk Profile:** low

## Description
Most bioavailable choline source, crossing the blood-brain barrier efficiently. Supports acetylcholine synthesis, cognitive function, and growth hormone release. Used clinically in Europe for cognitive decline and as a nootropic in the biohacking community.

## Mechanisms of Action
- Acetylcholine precursor
- Phosphatidylcholine synthesis
- Growth hormone potentiation
- Cell membrane integrity

## Dosage
- **Standard:** 300-600mg/day
- **Range:** 150-1200mg/day
- **Notes:** Take in morning or pre-workout. 600mg pre-exercise may boost GH. Pharmaceutical grade (Gliatilin) used in Europe for dementia. Hygroscopic â€” keep dry.

## Key Findings
- Improved cognitive function in Alzheimer patients in multiple Italian trials
- Acute 600mg dose increased growth hormone secretion
- Superior choline delivery to brain compared to choline bitartrate or CDP-choline

## Interactions
- Acetylcholinesterase inhibitors (additive effect)
- Scopolamine

## Side Effects
- Headache
- GI upset
- Insomnia if taken late
- Fishy body odor (rare, high doses)

## Contraindications
- Active peptic ulcer
