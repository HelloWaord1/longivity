# WHOOP Band

**Category:** device
**Evidence Grade:** B

## Description
Continuous physiological monitoring wearable focused on strain, recovery, and sleep. Uses HRV, resting heart rate, respiratory rate, and skin temperature to calculate daily recovery scores. Popular among athletes and biohackers for training optimization.

## Mechanisms of Action
- HRV-based recovery scoring
- Strain tracking (cardiovascular load)
- Sleep performance analysis
- Respiratory rate monitoring
