# GlyNAC (Glycine + NAC)

**Category:** protocol
**Evidence Grade:** B
**Risk Profile:** low

## Description
Restored glutathione levels in elderly to young levels

## Mechanisms of Action
- Glutathione restoration
- Mitochondrial function
- Oxidative stress reduction
- Insulin sensitivity

## Dosage
- **Standard:** Glycine 1.33mmol/kg + NAC 0.81mmol/kg daily
- **Range:** Typically ~7g glycine + ~5g NAC/day
- **Notes:** Baylor College study protocol. Simple, affordable, effective.

## Key Findings
- Restored glutathione levels in elderly to young levels
- Improved mitochondrial function, insulin resistance, inflammation
- Reversed multiple hallmarks of aging in randomized trial (Baylor 2023)
